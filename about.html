Our mission:

Titan Tickets aims to provide you with a random list of concerts in a city to give you an idea of the events in the area. The purpose is to explore artists you may have never considered. It may also inform you of your favorite artists you may of not known is going on tour!

What we offer:

Titan tickets lets you search by city anywhere in the world! Long are the days of having to dig for information regarding the event as we provide it on every card and direct you straight to the tickets link! If you love it you can favorite it and save for later. If you're ready to buy tickets we also made it easy for you to directlty go to the seatiing map and pick your seats out!

Another great feature is that if you love an artist we include recommendations of others you'll love too! This is a great place to explore music and see what's happening in the music world.
